# Privacy Policy — Pixel Mapper

_Last updated: 2026-06-22_

Pixel Mapper ("the app") is a tool for mapping LED pixel layouts into xLights
custom models. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your
information.

## Data we collect

**None.** The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking
SDKs.

## How the app uses device features

- **Camera** — used only on-device to photograph your light display and locate lit
  pixels with computer vision. Captured frames are processed locally and stored
  temporarily on the device to build your model. They are never uploaded.
- **Network / local connections** — the app sends pixel-control packets (DDP or
  sACN / E1.31) over your local network to the lighting controller you configure.
  No data is sent to the app's developer or any external server.
- **Local storage** — your settings (controller IP, pixel count, protocol,
  universe) are saved on the device. Exported `.xmodel` files are written where you
  choose to save them.

## Data sharing

The app does not share any data with third parties.

## Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children and collects no data from anyone.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same location
